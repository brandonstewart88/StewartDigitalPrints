Privacy Policy

Stewart Digital Prints · Last updated June 3, 2026

Stewart Digital Prints ("we," "us") operates a small digital art shop on Etsy (etsy.com/shop/StewartDigitalPrints) and uses a private, internal application to schedule and publish our own marketing content to our own Pinterest business account. This policy explains how that application handles data.

What the app does

The app creates and schedules Pinterest pins that link to our own Etsy listings. It is for our organization's personal use only. It is not offered to other users, and it does not manage, access, or publish to any Pinterest account other than our own.

Information we access

Through Pinterest's official API and the OAuth consent you grant, the app accesses only:

  • Our own Pinterest account profile and board list (to know where to post).
  • Permission to create pins on our own boards.

The app does not collect, store, or process personal information about Pinterest users, visitors, or third parties. It does not use cookies or tracking, and it does not sell or share any data.

How access tokens are stored

The OAuth access token issued by Pinterest is stored locally on our own computer in a private, non-shared configuration file and is used only to publish our pins. It is never transmitted to any third party.

Data retention & deletion

We retain only what is needed to operate the app (our token and board IDs). Access can be revoked at any time from your Pinterest account's connected-apps settings, which immediately ends the app's access.
